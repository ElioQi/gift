Privacy Policy for gift

Last Updated: 2023-10-27

1. Introduction

gift ("we", "our", or "us") operates a plugin for Coze that integrates with Pinterest. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application via Pinterest OAuth, we may access the following information based on the permissions you grant:

  • Your Pinterest profile information (username, profile image).
  • List of your Boards and Pins (if "boards:read" or "pins:read" scopes are granted).
  • Ability to create Boards or Pins (if "boards:write" or "pins:write" scopes are granted).

We do not collect any data outside of what is explicitly provided by the Pinterest API.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed is used solely to provide the functionality of the gift bot within the Coze platform, such as searching for images or saving pins.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to revoke access to our application at any time by visiting your Pinterest settings under "Apps and Websites".
